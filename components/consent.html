Study: Exploring Monsters

General Participant Information About the Study

Thank you for your interest in participating in this study conducted by Alexandr Ten, Michiko Sakaki, Kou Murayama, and collaborators at the Hector Institute for Educational Sciences and Psychology of the University of T√ºbingen. In this study, we will ask you to play a guessing game in which you will explore food preferences of fictional monsters. We will also ask you to fill out several questionnaires on your demographic characteristics and your beliefs. Some of you may be asked to complete a second part of the study [DELAY] hours later.

To participate in the experiment, you MUST meet the following criteria:

  • Be 18 years of age or older
  • Be a fluent or native speaker of [LANGUAGE]
  • Have never participated in this experiment before

The study will take [DURATION]. After completing the study, you will be reimbursed with [AMOUNT]/hour.

Voluntary Nature of the Study and Anonymity

We will identify you only by your Prolific ID. If you received a link to the study directly, we will ask you to provide an email address. These identifiers will be saved separately from the experimental data (e.g., demographic information, survey data, performance on the task). These identifiers will be saved separately from your experimental data and we will link your identifiers to your experimental data using an anonymized code. This code will be unrelated to your personal data and cannot be used to directly identify you. We will only use the code to match your personal data with your experimental data if you request that we delete your data and for the purposes of reimbursement. The coding list with your personal data will be stored away securely and deleted 60 days after we process payment for you. After this, your data will be rendered completely anonymous. The anonymized data will be stored for a minimum of 10 years. As long as the coding list exists, you may request that your data be deleted by reaching out to the research team (see contacts below).

Data Protection

Your participation in the study is completely voluntary. You may withdraw your participation from the study at any time without providing any reasons. Even if you do prematurely withdraw your participation, you are still entitled to compensation corresponding to the time you spent for the study. Data collected during the study and any personal communications we have will be treated confidentially. Project team members who have access to your personal data are also subject to confidentiality and data secrecy. Furthermore, the results of the study will be published anonymously, i.e., the data cannot trace back to you.

Consent

To participate in the experiment, you agree with the following statements:
  • I am aware that participation in the study is completely voluntary and that I can withdraw my participation at any time without providing any reasons. I will face no negative consequences if I withdraw my participation early.
  • I have read the information about data protection above and accept the conditions. I agree to have my data saved and will be completely anonymized two months after the completion of data collection.
  • I am aware that I have the right to request for my data to be deleted without providing any reasons. I have also been informed that I may contact the research team to have my data deleted before they become fully anonymized.
  • The data collected for the study will be analyzed at the University of T√ºbingen. I also consent that my anonymized data may be used for further research purposes and that the data will be stored for a minimum of 10 years at the University of T√ºbingen.
  • I consent for my data to be recorded for the purposes of the study and for their use for scientific purposes including the anonymous publication in scientific journals.
  • I agree that the anonymized data can be used in other research projects and can be accessible to other researchers on scientific platforms such as the Open Science Framework (www.osf.io).
  • I have had the time to read through the information about the study and data protection carefully and am ready to take part in the study.
