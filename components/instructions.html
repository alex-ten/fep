1. About the game

The game involves 3 families of food-picky monsters üëæ. Each family consists of various individuals, and each individual has its own unique appearance and favorite food.

While the monsters may not realize this, it is possible to predict what they prefer to eat by the way they look! The goal of the game is to figure out who likes to eat what based on how they look. In other words, you need to discover the rule behind food preferences of the monsters.

As you haven‚Äôt met the monsters yet, you can only guess what food preferences they have. However, you will get a chance to offer them food and see if they like it or not. These clues will help you figure out the rule!

2. Gameplay

When you interact with a monster family, different monsters from that family will be presented to you. For each monster, you will see two different food options.

You can indicate which food, in your opinion, is more likely to be enjoyed by the monster. To indicate your guess, you use the controls (slider and button) depicted below (more information on the next slide).

3.1. Controls

We will ask you to provide a guess for each monster.

For this, you will need to move the slider towards the option that you want to respond with.

For example, if you suspect - but not entirely sure - that a monster likes strawberries, you would move the slider closer towards the strawberry option as depicted below:

3.2. Controls

When you move the slider towards one of the options, you will see the Submit guess button change its color to green

You will also see a color bar appear under the selected option.

The color bar is there to indicate the level of confidence in your guess.

Please, indicate confidence in your guesses as truthfully as possible!

Note: you won't be able to proceed without providing a guess. The Submit guess button will be disabled whenever the slider is in the middle of the scale.
